Internal Medicine Training(IMT)

The Internal Medicine Training (IMT) program at KIMS Alshifa is accredited by the Federation of the Royal College of Physicians UK, ensuring high-quality education. It's a three-year postgraduate program equivalent to UK standards, allowing trainees to compete for higher specialty training in the UK. Trainees complete the MRCP exam and are exempt from PLAB for GMC registration. The curriculum focuses on developing essential clinical skills, professionalism, leadership, and teamwork. It offers diverse hospital, community, and academic experiences, building on previous training and allowing flexibility for academic pursuits.
